About

Star Wars Movie Guide is my portfolio project at ALX. I came up with this project becuase I was inspired by the Star Wars Franchise. I mean, who doesn't like to explore the universe? Star Wars films are admired and watched by millions in our universe (and many more in other galaxies...far far away :)). Form weird beings to driods and clones, I was immersed into a new and strange world when I first watched Attack of the Clones.

The film happened to be the first Star Wars film I ever watched and I was hooked. Of course I made sure I watched all of them and in the right order!. I then had an idea that probably one day I would create an app where not only I, but anyone else could easily access all the information related to Star Wars, and especially films. Thanks to ALX, I was finally able to implement it.
